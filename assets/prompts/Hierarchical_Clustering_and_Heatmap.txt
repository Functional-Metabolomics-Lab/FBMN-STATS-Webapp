This page displays **Hierarchical Clustering Analysis (HCA)** and a **clustered heatmap** of the metabolomics dataset.
- HCA is an **unsupervised** method that groups samples (or features) based on similarity.
- Clustering uses **Euclidean distance** and **complete linkage**.
- Results are shown as a **dendrogram** and a **heatmap** of feature intensities across samples.
- Similar samples or metabolites appear as blocks of similar colors.
- Users can choose from multiple **color-blind-friendly heatmap palettes**.
- Heatmaps may have limited label visibility due to high feature counts; zooming and hover are needed for detail.
- Outputs include the **clustered heatmap visualization** and the **underlying heatmap data table**.
